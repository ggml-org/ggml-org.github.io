ggml

AI at the edge

A tensor library for machine learning to enable large models and high performance on commodity hardware. Built for llama.cpp and whisper.cpp.

  • Cross-platform Low-level implementation
  • Quantization Integer quantization support
  • Hardware Broad hardware support
  • Dependencies None
  • Runtime allocations Zero

The ggml way

Minimal

We like simplicity and aim to keep the codebase as small and as simple as possible.

Company

ggml.ai was founded in 2023 by Georgi Gerganov to support the development of ggml. Nat Friedman and Daniel Gross provided the pre-seed funding. The company was acquired by Hugging Face in 2026.
